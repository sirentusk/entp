Compatibility

This page is showing details about ENTP (Ne-Ti-Fe-Si)'s relationship with all other types.

This page has writing, images, memes and videos. With a bonus section at the bottom for ENTP in love.

It is not meant to be relationship advice, but to explain why ENTP experiences people in certain ways.

To give a short summary of ENTP attractiveness. As an Ne dom, Ne is what ENTP thinks they predominantly are, which is pure creative chaos.

Ne is attracted to Ni - its yin/yang position, which is the organisation and regulation of chaos.

Therefore where Ni is positioned in another type's stack affects ENTPs attraction.

Enjoy the chaos below.
